A Stack Overflow survey found that 84% of developers use or plan to use AI tools, yet 46% distrust their accuracy when it comes to production code. The article walks through three practical risk checks before deploying AI-generated or vibe-coded work: understanding what the code actually does, assessing what systems it will access, and evaluating whether it is truly production-ready beyond an initial demo.
